About Us

History

Founded in 2016, C'Groovy is a well-known music streaming service. It provides a huge collection of music from many artists and genres worldwide. C'Groovy, which aims to make music accessible to everybody, lets users find, stream, and share songs that speak to them at any time or place. By utilizing cutting-edge technology, C'Groovy hopes to give its customers a smooth listening experience, tailored suggestions, and creative features that will foster a vibrant and engaging music community. C'Groovy has you covered whether you want to groove, unwind, or discover new tunes.
